## Usage

Use a verdict badge wherever a threat **verdict** needs a label — detail panels, table cells,
legends. Verdict is one of the system's three orthogonal channels and uses the dedicated
`--severity-*` palette, never the entity colors.

**When not to use:** on a graph node, the verdict is shown as a
**[VerdictPip](/docs/components/data-display/verdict-pip)** to stay compact.

## Accessibility

- The verdict text is the label — color is never the sole carrier of meaning.
- All severity colors meet AA against the badge surface in both themes.
