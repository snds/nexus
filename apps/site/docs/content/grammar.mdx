# Grammar & mechanics

## Capitalization

- **Sentence case** everywhere — buttons, headings, labels, menu items. "Save graph", not "Save
  Graph".
- Proper nouns and product names keep their case (Splunk, Nexus, TAP).

## Buttons & actions

- Start with a **verb**: "Save", "Delete", "Highlight path", "Add node".
- Be specific over generic where space allows: "Save graph" beats "OK".
- Destructive labels name the object: "Delete graph", not just "Delete".

## Numbers & units

- Numerals for all counts ("3 nodes", not "three nodes").
- Abbreviate large counts consistently (1.2k, 3.4M).
- Use the mono font (`--nx-font-mono`) for IDs, hashes, IPs, and timestamps.

## Punctuation

- No trailing periods on buttons, labels, or short single-line copy.
- Full sentences (error/empty messages) get periods.
- Use the Oxford comma.

## Dates & time

- Relative for recency ("2 hours ago"), absolute for precision (`2026-06-07 14:32 UTC`).
- Always make the absolute value available (tooltip or detail) for analytical accuracy.

## Truncation

- Truncate with an ellipsis and expose the full value on hover/focus — never drop information
  silently.
