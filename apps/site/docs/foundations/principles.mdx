# Principles

**Three orthogonal channels.** Color encodes entity *type*, shape encodes *family*, and verdict
lives on its own *pip*. They never collide — so a node reads at a glance and the legend stays
trustworthy.

**Fill is for active only.** Material Symbols are outlined by default; the FILL axis flips to 1
only for active / selected / hub states. Don't use fill for decoration.

**Token-sourced everything.** No component hardcodes a hue, blur, radius, or duration. Two
background contexts (canvas / panels), AA-safe text, and a dark-first ramp with a light override.

**Compose on Radix for generics.** Buttons, tooltips, dialogs, menus, and popovers are built on
shadcn/Radix bases — real focus management, escape, portals, and keyboard navigation — wrapped by
thin product primitives. Only the genuinely domain-specific graph pieces are bespoke.

**Motion that informs.** Entrances ease-out; new nodes expand from their parent; edges stay
locked to centers and never draw ahead of an arriving node. Everything honors
`prefers-reduced-motion`.

**Accessibility is enforced, not aspirational.** Every Storybook story is rendered through axe in
CI; structural/ARIA failures break the build.
