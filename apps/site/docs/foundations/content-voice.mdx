# Content & voice

How the system *writes* is part of how it's designed. The full guidance — voice & tone, grammar &
mechanics, and the threat-domain terminology — lives in its own section:

- **[Content & voice → Voice & tone](/docs/content/voice-tone)**
- **[Content & voice → Grammar & mechanics](/docs/content/grammar)**
- **[Content & voice → Terminology](/docs/content/terminology)**

## In short

- **Calm and precise.** This is a security tool — copy is direct, never alarmist. State the fact;
  let the verdict color do the rest.
- **The user is the analyst.** Address them plainly; assume domain fluency without being terse.
- **Consistent terms.** "verdict", "entity", "campaign", "hub" mean exactly one thing — see the
  [terminology](/docs/content/terminology).
