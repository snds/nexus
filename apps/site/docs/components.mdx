# Components

The library has two tiers. **L1 base** are pristine shadcn/Radix primitives (Button, Tooltip,
Dialog, DropdownMenu, Popover) — explore their variants and interactive (open) states in
**[Storybook → Base](/nexus/storybook/?path=/story/base-button--all-variants)**. **L3 product
primitives** compose them with tokens; live examples follow.
